Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Here’s how we handle your data:

  • We collect your Telegram user ID to initialize the app.
  • We store your game progress (coins) in your browser’s local storage.
  • We do not share your data with third parties, except for ad providers (Monetag).
  • TON wallet data is handled by TON Connect and not stored by us.
